About This Project

This archive presents verified Goldbach decompositions for even numbers up to selected large bounds. It is based on a method I developed and validated, allowing decomposition into two primes even beyond 1018 in some cases.

My goal is to provide clear, simple, and public access to this number theory result through well-structured archives, without relying on heavy computation at runtime.

The method, now named Goldbach-GPS, is my own original work and is made publicly available for educational and scientific purposes.
